Living Worlds

Virtual Worlds

Have you ever wanted to walk through the halls of Moria or experience the dark dread of standing at the base of Dol Guldur? Have you ever wanted to explore the Star Ship Enterprise or the vast expanse of Red Dwarf?

Headsets such as the Meta Quest 2 allow us to model out and reproduce iconic places and scenes to explore. We are pulling together maps and schematics for many of the most iconic places in literature and film and rebuilding them for Meta Quest and other headsets.
