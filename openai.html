OpenAI

Emersive AI

Building virtual worlds presents two challenges. Firstly, building worlds is time consuming. Secondly, those built worlds can be sparsely populated while the NPCs we encounter can spoil the emmersive effect of being in that world.

We are researching the application of OpenAI to assist with the creation of new virtual worlds and for populating those worlds with characters that live and feel true to the environment.
