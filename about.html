About

We Are Building New Experiences

We are a collaboration of projects and teams who are all passionate about building new consumer experiences built on new technology including Virtual Reality, OpenAI, Blockchain and Film. Our passion is the human experience. Our mission is to bring together our shared expertise to build projects that we can take to the world, to inspire and touch peoples lives.

Current Teams and Projects

We are starting with teams and projects that already align with our shared vision.

Catalyst is a project that started its life in June 2018, looking at how to build a blockchain and distributed file system for supporting media projects.

Jeskei is a decentralised film and media project that wraps media as NFTs and allows content creators to earn fairly from what they create.

New Projects

We are excited about a series of new initiatives.

Living Worlds - Targeting Meta Quest, we are building new emersive experiences set in popular locations from the mines of Moria to the Starship Enterprise to Red Dwarf.

OpenAI - We are exploring the application of OpenAI to accelerate the building of new environments as well as building a more engaging experience for users.

Popups - 4 to 8 Meta VR headsets networked for friends to experience VR and our latest creations while providing an extra income to the project.
