Popups

VR on the road

Virtual worlds are fun but can be even more fun when experienced with friends. The aim of our popuaps programme is to take VR on the road and to allow people to experience the power of VR for themselves and with friends.

Hire between 4 and 8 headsets at a popup venue along with accessories to experience a fun packed special event. Want to play a golf tournament with friends? Want to explore Moria as part of a Tolkien special event experience? Want to just play some games with friends? There's nothing more fun than a VR team experience. We will reinvest earnings from our popup experiences to create better future experiences and more popups.

While revenue generating, a byproduct is the opportunity to grow brand awareness while showing off what the wider Metaverse Studios are building. This creates an in-person sales channel and marketing, helping to grow community and sales opportunities.
