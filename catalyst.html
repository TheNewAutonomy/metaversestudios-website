Catalyst

CATALYST NETWORK

Catalyst is a research project that started in 2018 to look at how to combine blockchain and distributed file storage for the purpose of supporting future media including film, gaming and VR. A total of $5 million was raised from private investors to fund this research from conception, through technical research and publications through to the launch of a public test network. Catalyst is built on .NET to simplify the integration of frameworks such as Unity.

With the launch of a public network, further work was suspended until a market and plan could be found to bring Catalyst to market in a production environment. With projects such as Jeskei combining crypto payments, film assets and on-chain communities; We are looking to update Catalyst to the latest EVM standards then launch it as a new network for media projects.
