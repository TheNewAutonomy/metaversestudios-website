Jeskei

JESKEI MEDIA

Jeskei is a project born out of the combination of two historic challenges facing the film and media industry. The first is that actors and other contributors in the film industry are locked out of revenue sharing opportunities for the films that they produce. How do we know? Because many of the biggest stars in film and TV have been campaigning to find ways that technology could enable creatives and performers to fairly earn a share of the takings from the films they give so much passion to. While this issue impacts all performers and creatives in the film industry, its also a challenge impacting new video services such as YouTube where content creators earn very little of the revenue being generated by their content.

Secondly, that film distribution continues to be costly and risky endeavour. The Jeskei team built a film distribution platform by networking datacentres to streamline the distribution process from studio to cinemas. However, while a technical success, the datacentres extracted all the profit making it an impractical solution financially for studios and cinemas.

Jeskei cracks these challenges by combining distributed file storage with blockchain. Film assets are represented as NFTs while pay per view and subscription services are automatically distributed to contributors via smart contracts. This is fair and transparent and allows any contributor to a project to earn a fair take. A byproduct is that content creators and consumers are brought closer together, allowing fans to invest in new productions while allowing new digital studios to create more high risk and niche content.
